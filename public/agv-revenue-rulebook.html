Official Ticket Revenue Rulebook

AGV ticketed events use a transparent revenue model. Gross ticket revenue is reduced by AGV’s fixed 7% ticket platform fee, any applicable broadcast delivery fee, and payment processing fees. The remaining balance is the host or vendor net revenue.

Official AGV Ticket Formula
Gross Ticket Revenue Total ticket sales before deductions
- AGV 7% Platform Fee AGV’s fixed fee for ticketed event access and platform monetization tools
- Broadcast Delivery Fee Streaming / broadcast usage cost, separate from AGV’s platform fee
- Payment Processing Fee Stripe / card processing cost
= Host / Vendor Net Revenue Final amount owed after fee deductions
This structure keeps AGV’s platform fee separate from broadcast delivery and card processing. AGV earns the platform fee, broadcast delivery covers streaming usage, processing covers the payment provider, and the host or vendor receives the true net amount.

What each revenue line means

Gross Ticket Revenue

The full amount collected from ticket sales before fees are deducted. Example: 100 tickets at $25 equals $2,500 gross ticket revenue.

AGV 7% Platform Fee

AGV’s fixed ticket platform fee. This supports AGV’s ticketed event system, platform access, event controls, and monetization structure.

Broadcast Delivery Fee

This fee is separate from AGV’s 7% platform fee and is used to cover streaming and broadcast delivery usage. Large events may require a custom quote.

Payment Processing Fee

This is the card/payment provider fee. It is separate from AGV’s platform fee and should be passed through as part of the event’s final revenue calculation.

Host Net Revenue

The final event revenue owed to the host after the AGV platform fee, broadcast delivery fee, and payment processing fee are deducted.

Vendor Net Revenue

The final vendor revenue owed to a vendor after applicable AGV platform fees, booth-ticket fees, delivery costs, and processing fees are deducted.

Revenue policy rules for launch

Transparent Fee Separation

  • AGV platform fee is fixed at 7% of gross ticket revenue.
  • Broadcast delivery is a separate usage-based or quoted cost.
  • Payment processing is separate and passed through.

Large Event Quote

  • Convention-scale events may require a custom broadcast delivery quote.
  • High-traffic events should be reviewed before launch.
  • Large audiences should not be priced blindly.

Refund / Dispute Notice

  • Refunds, cancellations, chargebacks, and disputes require a written AGV policy.
  • Host payouts may be held during disputes or manual review.
  • Unpaid or failed payments should never create valid access.
