Founder Mission

AGV was built to give everyday people a professional stage.

Avant Global Vision was created for people and organizations who have something valuable to share, but need a safer, more professional way to present it, organize it, and monetize it.

AGV is more than a streaming page. It is a digital venue where churches can reach beyond the sanctuary, teachers can grow beyond the classroom, podcasters can own their audience, creators can sell access, nonprofits can raise support, and convention hosts can bring people together from anywhere.

“The ticketed system was created to give everyday people a way to safely display their skills and monetize it.”
— Founder Byron Avant Sr.

AGV gives everyday people and organizations a safer, more professional way to present, connect, and monetize.

2Set up your venue

3Invite or sell access

4Broadcast and grow
