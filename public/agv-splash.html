How AGV works

From the first event idea to the final revenue report, AGV gives hosts one connected path for planning, access, broadcasting, engagement, monitoring, and growth.

2 Build the venue

3 Invite or sell access

4 Broadcast and engage

5 Monitor with Sentinel

6 Review and grow

Examine revenue, event performance, vendor activity, and lessons that can strengthen the next experience.
